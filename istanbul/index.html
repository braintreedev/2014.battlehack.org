Piggy Bank

App for parents and their kids to help control their spending meanwhile supporting an healthy, active lifestyle; using PayPal 'student accounts' and iBeacon technologies.

Runners up

FitPal

2nd Place

FitPal is a fitness application, that motivates you to make your 7-minute workout everyday and checks activity with JawBone Up. Otherwise it donates $1 to Aspire charity.

WouldPay

3rd Place

An application that lets users set prices they are willing to pay for items online and get notifications when or if the price ever drops down to that amount.
