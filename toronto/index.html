Parkle

3rd Place

Parkle allows you to easily pay for parking using PayPal on your mobile phone. The app will direct you to a parking lot of your choice, and once you’ve parked will prompt you to start the parking meter right from your phone. When it’s time for you to leave, Parkle will automatically detect when you return to your car, and complete the transaction. Parking made simple.
