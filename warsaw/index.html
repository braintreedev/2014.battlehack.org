About the Winning Hackers

Catena

We want to build a hardware solution that creates an open trusted network marketplace for listing, discovering and sharing electrical devices. Lets say you want to share a coffee maker with your coworkers, a working spot in a hacklab or a 3D printing machine, you just need to place Catena in the power cord and its ready to be shared. The prototype will be made using raspberry pi, wifi, ibeacon, relays and on the software side node.js, angular, HTML5, redis/mongo and a native android app. We are looking for web developers and designers, join us! or just come to say hi! or not :)

PartyPay

3rd Place

Do you want to throw an awesome party and you don't want to go bankrupt? GroupPay is for you! Our project let's you easily invite your friends to a party and charge them with a small payment. Organiser can spare it on drinks and food and if there's something left it can be easily divided among contributors or giveaway to charity.

Warsaw Highlights

We hosted our first BattleHack in Warsaw, Poland and after 24 hours of stiff competition, 22 teams presented their hacks to our esteemed panel of judges. The hack took place at Nowy Fort, an ancient fort in the old district of Warsaw and was filled with BattleHack 2013 alums back for another round.
