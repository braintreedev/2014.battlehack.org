About the Winning Hackers

Gearbox

Our aim is to help get people out and about by encouraging the use of sports equipment at parks. Its an app that is an IoT based solution that uses a mobile application to open a secure box to access sports equipment which is payed for using PayPal.

3rd Place

The Big Issue is a not-for-profit organisation that creates job opportunities and a a sense of independence in homeless/marginalised/disadvantaged people all over Australia. We propose to provide vendors with bluetooth tokens and an app that recognises when you are in range and allows you to purchase it without the need for cash.
